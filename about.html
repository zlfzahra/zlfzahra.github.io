Researcher in remote sensing, Earth observation, and environmental informatics.

Biography

I am Zalfa Afifah Zahra, a master's graduate in Civil Engineering and Environmental Informatics at Minghsin University of Science and Technology, Taiwan. My research interests include satellite remote sensing, environmental monitoring, and machine learning for climate and water-related applications.

My recent work includes harmful algal bloom detection across reservoirs in Taiwan, urban heat island forecasting using Landsat and meteorological data, and sea surface temperature rise detection using satellite imagery.
